Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

Our Promise

We believe in privacy, not surveillance. We collect only what’s needed to provide our services, and we never sell or trade your data. Your trust is at the core of everything we build.

What We Collect

  • Tag and item information (e.g. item type, description)
  • Contact details you choose to share (e.g. email, phone number)
  • Finder submissions (messages, photos, location – if you choose to share it)
  • Basic technical info (browser type, approximate location – for debugging only)

What We Don't Do

  • We do not track your movement or browsing
  • We do not profile, resell, or advertise to you
  • We do not store payment details on our servers

Why We Collect It

  • To help return lost items to their rightful owners
  • To notify you if someone finds your item
  • To maintain and improve the reliability of our service

Your Control

  • You can delete your data at any time
  • You can choose how much or how little to share
  • Finder contact details are automatically deleted after 7 days (Mate.ID rule)

Security

We use modern encryption and data minimization techniques to protect your data. All data is stored securely, and only essential staff can access system-level information for troubleshooting.

International Use

This service is operated from Australia but designed to respect privacy worldwide. We comply with Australia’s Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).
